Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Powel Mwenesi with an ambitious dream, Akubrecah Entertainment began to provide affordable, high-quality digital and printing services while fostering creativity and innovation in branding and design to bring modern digital services to the heart of Kapenguria, West Pokot.

Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality digital and printing services that empower creativity and innovation in branding and design.

Vision

To become the leading cybercafe and digital solutions provider in Kapenguria and Kenya at Large, empowering businesses and Individuals with cutting-edge technology.
